short guide

Obtaining stock data
1) Enter the stock ticker in field "ticker"
2) Enter a briefcase in the field "portfel"
3) Enter the start and end dates of the saved period. You can use the calendar.
4) Click the Start button.

You will receive:
1) Chart of a specified period with marked anomalies in the stock price
2) Information about each anomaly
3) News summary for a given stock



Obtaining cryptocurrency data
1)Enter the cryptocurrency ticker in the field "ticker"
2)Field "portfel" does not need to be filled in
3)Enter the start and end dates of the saved period. You can use the calendar.
4)Click the Start button

You will receive:
1) Chart for a given period with marked anomalies in the price of cryptocurrency
2) Information about each of the anomalies
3) News summary for a given cryptocurrency
Subscribe to notifications
If you want to receive notifications about anomalies in real time, you need to create an account. To do this, click on the profile button to the login page, and from there to the registration page. After you enter your email, a password will be sent to it. Using it, log into your account. To subscribe to notifications, you should tick the "I want to receive notifications about changes" option when entering information on the main page. If you want to unsubscribe from any of the stocks or cryptocurrencies, you can do this in your profile. Also there you can log out of your account or delete it.
